Project GCF

Project Info:

The Easy Harvest Manager (GCF) application was developed in order to facilitate the management of crops in a simple way! You can register several cultures that work with weight (Kg), units (Un.) or even bags (60Kg). In addition to registering the vehicles that carry out the freight and the silos or warehouses where their products were delivered. The application also allows you to control your sales, making it possible to be aware of payment dates, quantities sold, value sold and among others.Available just in Brazil. In process of translation for English and come soon in Apple Store and Play Store for all world.
